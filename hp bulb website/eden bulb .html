EDEN BULB INFORMATION

Eden Bulb International Nursery, Primary and High School is a vibrant, faith-based institution dedicated to nurturing excellence in every child. As a missionary school founded and opernated under the visionary leadership of Hosanna praise bulb International Church our mission is rooted in spiritual growth, academic excellence, and holistic development.
